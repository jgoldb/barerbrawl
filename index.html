Each hall is sealed once you enter. Lay out every bochur to open the far gate. The yeshiva has no end — only how deep you get before the chevra buries you. Keep your combo alive for score, and grab a dropped kugel to heal.
On a phone or tablet: left thumb steers, drag the right to look, and tap the buttons to brawl. Push the stick all the way to sprint.
